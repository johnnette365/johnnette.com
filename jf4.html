Johnnette JF-4



Johnnette JF-4 is a Multi-role Unmanned Combat Aerial Vehicle (UCAV) which has the capability of conducting Intelligence, Surveillance and Reconnaissance (ISR) and armed attack missions. Johnnette JF-4 combines an impressive performance envelope with extended operational coverage and radically reduced logistical footprint. Johnnette JF-4 is designed to operate with, and support the higher tactical echelon, providing intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition, and reconnaissance. This system will carry payloads up to 10 kg in weight, including multi-sensor stabilized EO payloads.


Design

Johnnette JF-4 will be designed to operate with, and support the higher tactical echelon, providing intelligence, surveillance, target acquisition, and reconnaissance. This system will carry payloads up to 10 kg in weight, including multi-sensor stabilized EO payloads.


Ground Control Station

Johnnette JF-4 is controlled from a command control station in the manual, semi-automatic and automatic modes. The control system allows performing an autonomous flight along the desired track, which can consist of both geographic and relative coordinates. The UAV is capable of logging basic flight parameters and transfer the logged data to the ground control station for displaying the data on the pilot’s viewscreen and recording it to a log-file.
