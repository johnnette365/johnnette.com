Johnnette JF-3 VTOL



The Johnnette JF-3 VTOL is a vertical take-off and landing (VTOL), fixed-wing unmanned aircraft used to provide advanced multi-sensor intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance (ISR) support. Delivering 3+ hours of endurance and an operational range of 50 km, the Johnnette JF-3 VTOL is ideal for multi-mission operations. Runway independent, the system can be set up and operational in less than 15 minutes without the need for launch or recovery equipment. With a useable payload capacity of up to 5 kg, the Johnnette JF-3 VTOL offers flexible payload options.


Design

Launch, Anytime, Anywhere. The ultra-portable VTOL launch and recovery system eliminates the need for bulky or expensive equipment, enabling rapid deployment from any terrain in less than 15 minutes.


Multi Mission Payload Options

With the ability to carry up to 5 Kg of payload, our multi domain solution provide a level of integrated ISR data previously unavailable in a tactical, long endurance UAS platform. Purpose built for maximum payload integration flexibility; the Johnnette JF-3 VTOL is configurable to meet changing user requirements.
