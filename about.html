Johnnette Technologies has been a leading provider of tactical Unmanned Aerial Vehicles and Unmanned Ground Vehicles for the defence and application oriented products for commercial sector. We are a ISO certified company with highly experienced veterans and industry experts. We have been delivering products that solve problems that otherwise seem impossible.

Johnnette Technologies Pvt Ltd. (ISO 9001:2008 certified) has been a pioneer in the development of Unmanned Systems Technology in India. The company was registered in April 2014 and since then the company has been providing cutting edge high-quality aerospace systems to the paramilitary, commercial, aerospace and defence sectors. Our expertise in the Unmanned Systems Technology has helped to develop cutting edge Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) and Unmanned Ground Vehicles (UGVs) that are capable of carrying out tasks that are impossible otherwise by humans. With a military qualified expert team, Johnnette has provided various solutions to the military and commercial sectors. Johnnette Technologies was awarded the Best Technology Product Startup of the Year at SEDI Awards 2015, Industrial Excellence Award in 2017, Top 200 MSME Award in 2019 and Industrial Product Finder Award in 2020.
